Friend Network

How to interpret this diagram:
  • Larger circles mean more games played with the person
  • Thicker lines connecting the circles represent more games played between players
    • Any line connecting you to your friends is omitted from display
  • Colors are random
  • Two people who share similar friend groups are located close together
  • Any person you've played with 3 or more times has a circle. Others are not displayed.
  • Only the top 200 friends you've played the most with will be displayed on this network graph.
